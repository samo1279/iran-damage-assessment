User Guide -- Iran Damage Assessment Platform v5.0

What is this?

A real-time intelligence fusion platform that combines Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) from global news feeds with satellite imagery analysis to assess damage to strategic targets in Iran during the ongoing conflict.

How does it work?

  • OSINT Engine: Continuously scans the GDELT global news database for reports of strikes, explosions, and military activity in Iran. Articles are matched against our database of known targets.
  • Target Database: Contains 180+ known military, nuclear, missile, air defense, energy, and government targets across Iran, organized by province.
  • Satellite Analysis: Downloads Sentinel-2 optical imagery (10m resolution) to compare before/after conditions at target sites. Change detection uses pixel differencing + NDVI analysis.
  • Correlation Engine: Fuses OSINT reports with satellite evidence to produce a confidence verdict: CONFIRMED, LIKELY, REPORTED, or UNCONFIRMED.

Understanding the Map

  • Red markers: Confirmed or likely strikes with high confidence
  • Orange markers: Reported strikes with moderate confidence
  • Blue markers: Known targets in database (no strike reported yet)
  • City labels: White text labels show major city names for orientation
  • Target labels: Yellow text labels show target names
  • Click any marker to see details and evidence

Understanding Confidence Levels

  • CONFIRMED (80-100%): Multiple OSINT sources + satellite imagery shows visible change
  • LIKELY (50-79%): Multiple news reports corroborate the strike, satellite may show changes
  • REPORTED (20-49%): News reports mention the target but limited corroboration
  • UNCONFIRMED (0-19%): Single mention or indirect reference only

Tabs

  • STRIKES: All reported strikes sorted by confidence. Each card shows the probability, verdict, and sources.
  • TARGETS: Browse all 180+ targets in the database. Filter by category, province, or search by name.
  • NEWS: Latest OSINT articles from global news feeds. Each shows relevance probability to military strikes.
  • SATELLITE: Select a target to fetch and compare before/after satellite imagery.

Automatic Refresh

The platform automatically scans for new intelligence every 5 minutes. The "Last Scan" indicator in the stats bar shows when the last refresh occurred. All data loads automatically on page open -- no buttons needed.

Data Sources

  • GDELT Project -- Global news monitoring (free, no API key)
  • Sentinel-2 L2A -- 10m optical imagery via Element84 STAC (free)
  • Sentinel-1 SAR -- 10m radar imagery (free)
  • PlanetScope -- 3m imagery (requires API key in settings)
